Covid-19 DB Project


OBJECTIVE:

We are a group of scientists, medical researchers, statisticians and engineers. In response to the current coronavirus outbreak, we are creating a comprehensive, publicly available database of the severe acute respiratory syndrome coronavirus 2 (SARS-CoV-2) that causes coronavirus disease (COVID-19). This would allow us to create detailed visualisation tools, build a model of the global spread of the virus and potentially inform adequate public policy strategy.
